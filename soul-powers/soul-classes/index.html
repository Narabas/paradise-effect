Soul Classes

Every Soul has a color, and each color designates abilities that that soul can utilize in their everyday lives, for a variety of reasons, as well as a general stereotype of those who share that Soul Class with one another. How true the stereotype is, is up to individual interpretation.

Guessing your character's Soul Class will come during Chapter 01. You will be prompted when the time comes, so don't worry about missing out!


Purple Soul

Purple Souls are beings of ice and space. They rely on their own mental acuity for their powers, which means the more intelligence one has, the stronger they are when they awaken their soul. Although there are many facets to being a Purple Soul, by and large they're considered to be clever and observant cats, with a complex and aloof side most try to avoid. They have a tendency to act on their own, without taking into account the thoughts and feelings of others.

Red Soul

Red Souls are beings of fire and earth. They rely on their own physicality for their powers, which means the more strength one has, the stronger they are when they awaken their soul. Although there are many facets to being a Red Soul, by and large they're considered to be passionate and friendly cats, with an aggressive and hot-headed side most try to avoid. They have a tendency to stand their ground with an unrelenting, stubborn resolve.

Yellow Soul

Yellow Souls are beings of plasma and transformation. They rely on their own vitality for their powers, which means the more constitution one has, the stronger they are when they awaken their soul. Although there are many facets to being a Yellow Soul, by and large they're considered to be hard-working and adaptable cats, with an overbearing and unpredictable side most try to avoid. They have a tendency to advise and guide others, sometimes to an almost controlling degree.

Green Soul

Green Souls are beings of wind and growth. They rely on their own worldly understanding for their powers, which means the more wisdom one has, the stronger they are when they awaken their soul. Although there are many facets to being a Green Soul, by and large they're considered to be selfless and playful cats, with a meek and non-committal side most try to avoid. They have a tendency to become martyrs in times of strife.

Blue Soul

Blue Souls are beings of water and illusion. They rely on their own social tact for their powers, which means the more charisma one has, the stronger they are when they awaken their soul. Although there are many facets to being a Blue Soul, by and large they're considered to be calm and diplomatic cats, with a whimsical and cautious side most try to avoid. They have a tendency to be cool under pressure, which makes them naturally inclined to leadership positions.

Black Soul

Black Souls are beings of shadows and decay. They rely on their own finesse for their powers, which means the more dexterity one has, the stronger they are when they awaken their soul. Although there are many facets to being a Black Soul, by and large they're considered to be independent and thoughtful cats, with a short-sighted and scheming side most try to avoid. They have a tendency to see matters in absolute, black and white terms.
