Yellow Soul

Powers: Plasma & Transformation

Stat: Constitution

Alignments: Red & Green

Side Effects: Due to the overbearing and meddling nature of Yellow Souls, they can be prone to controlling those around them and rejecting any authority that is not their own, especially at higher levels. They're known to use whatever means necessary to get their way.

For duration-based attacks, 5 minutes pass per post by the character(s) affected by the power. So, for instance, if Character A is using a 15 minute attack on Character B, then the effects of the attack will last until Character B has made 3 posts since being attacked by Character A's power.

Duration-based abilities that aren't attacks (ex. flying) follow these same post rules, except the 5 minutes pass per post by the user!

Level 0

(Entry Stat 0 - 1)

Powers are inward-focused.
Abilities:
  • They can make their fur glow at will.
    • Limitations: Cannot yet manipulate plasma or transform. Can only glow as bright as a 60 watt lightbulb.

Level 1

(Entry Stat 2 - 3)

Abilities:
  • They can create bright flashes of light to blind others.
    • Limitations: Can only cause temporary blindness for up to 5 minutes max. If the target has already been deafened then they cannot also be blinded.

  • They can generate an electric buzzing noise to deafen others.
    • Limitations: Can only cause temporary deafness for up to 5 minutes max. If the target has already been blinded then they cannot also be deafened.

  • They can start learning how to transform.
    • Limitations: They can only temporarily change the colors and patterns of their fur. Transformations can last for 3 hours max.

Level 2

(Entry Stat 4 - 5)

Abilities:
  • They can create bright flashes of light to blind others.
    • Limitations: Can only cause temporary blindness for up to 10 minutes max. If the target has already been deafened then they cannot also be blinded.

  • They can generate an electric buzzing noise to deafen others.
    • Limitations: Can only cause temporary deafness for up to 10 minutes max. If the target has already been blinded then they cannot also be deafened.

  • They can start to generate small, powerful shocks of lightning.
    • Limitations: These bolts can only reach within a 5 foot (1.5 meter) radius from their body. It's strong enough to leave a burn, or to stun creatures less than half the user's size.

  • They can begin perfecting their ability to transform, including changing into other domestic feline shapes.
    • Limitations: They can only temporarily change the colors and patterns of their fur, and/or their size to be 2x bigger or 2x smaller than their usual size. Transformations can last for 6 hours max.

Level 3

(Entry Stat 6 - 7)

Abilities:
  • They can create bright flashes of light to blind others.
    • Limitations: Can only cause temporary blindness for up to 20 minutes max. If the target has already been deafened then they cannot also be blinded.

  • They can generate an electric buzzing noise to deafen others.
    • Limitations: Can only cause temporary deafness for up to 20 minutes max. If the target has already been blinded then they cannot also be deafened.

  • They can start to generate powerful shocks of lightning.
    • Limitations: These bolts can only reach within a 10 foot (1.5 meter) radius from their body, or 20 feet (6.1 meters) straight out. It's strong enough to leave a nasty burn or stun creatures up to 2x the user's size, or to kill creatures less than half the user's size.

  • They can start to manipulate lightning into shapes, or hold it in the palm of their paw.
    • Limitations: They can't hold/manipulate amounts larger than one fourth of their own size.

  • They can change their entire body into a different domestic feline shape, and start to transform others.
    • Limitations: They can only transform up to 2 willing cats at a time, and can only temporarily change these cats' colors and patterning for up to 6 hours at a time. They can transform themselves for up to 12 hours at a time.

Level 4

(Entry Stat 8 - 9)

Abilities:
  • They can generate powerful shocks of lightning, even calling them from the sky if they choose.
    • Limitations: These bolts can only reach within a 15 foot (4.6 meter) radius from their body, or 35 feet (11 meters) straight out. It's strong enough to kill creatures up to twice their size or smaller.

  • They can manipulate lightning into shapes, or hold it in the palm of their paw.
    • Limitations: They can't hold/manipulate amounts larger than one half of their own size.

  • They can change their entire body into a different domestic feline shape, and start to transform others.
    • Limitations: They can only transform up to 2 willing cats at a time, and can only temporarily change these cats' colors and patterning and/or size to 2x bigger or smaller for up to 12 hours at a time. They can transform themselves for up to 1 day max at a time.

  • They can begin to enhance their regular abilities by taking on sensory traits (eyes, ears, limbs, fur/skin, etc.) of another animal.
    • Limitations: They can only take on up to 2 traits at a time, and it only lasts for 10 minutes max.

Level 5

(Entry Stat 10)

Abilities:
  • They can generate powerful shocks of lightning, even calling them from the sky if they choose.
    • Limitations: These bolts can only reach within a 20 foot (6.1 meter) radius from their body, or 50 feet (15 meters) straight out. It's strong enough to kill creatures up to the size of an average horse.

  • They can manipulate lightning into shapes, or hold it in the palm of their paw.
    • Limitations: They can't hold/manipulate amounts larger than their own size.

  • They can change their entire body, and the entire body of others, into a different domestic feline shape.
    • Limitations: They can only transform up to 2 willing cats at a time, and can only temporarily change these cats for up to 12 hours at a time. They can transform themselves for up to 2 days max at a time.

  • They can enhance their regular abilities by taking on sensory traits (eyes, ears, limbs, fur/skin, etc.) of another animal.
    • Limitations: They can only take on up to 2 traits at a time, and it only lasts for 30 minutes max.
