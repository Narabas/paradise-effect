Purple Soul

Powers: Ice & Space

Stat: Intelligence

Alignments: Black & Red

Side Effects: Due to the distant and aloof nature of Purple Souls, they can be prone to closing themselves off and acting without regard for others, especially at higher levels. They're known to choose self-survival over empathy or compassion.

For duration-based attacks, 5 minutes pass per post by the character(s) affected by the power. So, for instance, if Character A is using a 15 minute attack on Character B, then the effects of the attack will last until Character B has made 3 posts since being attacked by Character A's power.

Duration-based abilities that aren't attacks (ex. flying) follow these same post rules, except the 5 minutes pass per post by the user!

Level 0

(Entry Stat 0 - 1)

Powers are inward-focused.
Abilities:
  • They can cool their body to icy temperatures at will.
    • Limitations: Cannot yet manipulate ice or space. Can only lower their body temperature down to 20 degrees F (-6.7 C).

Level 1

(Entry Stat 2 - 3)

Abilities:
  • They can gather frost on the ends of their whiskers and fur.
    • Limitations: They cannot make frost form on a surface outside of themselves.

  • They can generate small shards of ice that can be hurled at others.
    • Limitations: They can only create up to 5 small shards at a time, no bigger than their own claws.

  • They can start to sense when things are amiss around them.
    • Limitations: They cannot always pinpoint the source of the feeling, just that something is wrong. They are around 60% accurate.

Level 2

(Entry Stat 4 - 5)

Abilities:
  • They can coat parts of themselves in ice, and can make frost form on other surfaces.
    • Limitations: They can only turn their claws and teeth to ice, and nothing bigger.

  • They can generate spears of ice that can be hurled at others.
    • Limitations: They can only create spears up to half of their own size, and can only create up to 3 spears at a time.

  • They can begin to sense the shape of small objects without seeing them, and can affect their gravitational weight.
    • Limitations: Can only sense the shape of objects less than 10 lbs (4.5 kg), and can only affect their weight for a few seconds at a time.

  • They can start to sense trustworthy and untrustworthy individuals.
    • Limitations: They can only use this power on one living being at a time, and are only around 60% accurate. They also can't pinpoint the reason to trust or distrust.

Level 3

(Entry Stat 6 - 7)

Abilities:
  • They can coat parts of themselves in ice, and can make ice form on other surfaces.
    • Limitations: They can coat up to two of their own limbs in ice, or the claws and teeth of other cats or similar-sized creatures.

  • They can generate spears of ice that can be hurled at others.
    • Limitations: They can only create spears up to 3x their own size, and can only create up to 3 spears at a time.

  • They can breathe out flakes of snow, and generate snow showers.
    • Limitations: They can only create snow showers within a 10 foot (3.1 meter) radius around themselves.

  • They can begin to sense the shape of medium-sized objects without seeing them, and can affect their gravitational weight.
    • Limitations: Can only sense the shape of objects less than 25 lbs (11 kg), and can only affect their weight for 15 minutes at a time.

  • They can start to sense when something around them is an illusion.
    • Limitations: They cannot accurately deduce the truth except through logic. They are only around 75% accurate.

Level 4

(Entry Stat 8 - 9)

Abilities:
  • They can coat parts of themselves in ice, and can make ice form on other surfaces.
    • Limitations: They can coat up to half of themselves in ice, or up to two limbs of other cats or similar-sized creatures.

  • They can generate spears of ice that can be hurled at others, including their own blood.
    • Limitations: They can only create spears up to several times their own size, and can only create up to 5 spears at a time.

  • They can breathe out flakes of snow, and generate snow showers and ice storms.
    • Limitations: They can only create snow showers or ice storms within a 15 foot (4.6 meter) radius around themselves.

  • They can begin to sense the shape of large-sized objects without seeing them, and can affect their gravitational weight, and/or move them around at will.
    • Limitations: Can only sense the shape of objects less than 50 lbs (22 kg), and can only affect their weight or move them for 30 minutes at a time.

  • They can start to learn how to teleport.
    • Limitations: They cannot teleport more than a half mile between locations, and the destination has to be a location they can see or sense. Limited to 3 uses per Chapter.

Level 5

(Entry Stat 10)

Abilities:
  • They can coat themselves in ice, and can make ice form on other surfaces.
    • Limitations: They can coat all of themselves in ice, or up to half the body of other cats or similar-sized creatures.

  • They can generate spears of ice that can be hurled at others, including their own blood.
    • Limitations: They can only create spears up to several times their own size, and can only create up to 7 spears at a time.

  • They can breathe out flakes of snow, and generate snow showers and ice storms.
    • Limitations: They can only create snow showers or ice storms within a 20 foot (6.1 meter) radius around themselves.

  • They can begin to sense the shape of very large-sized objects without seeing them, and can affect their gravitational weight, and/or move them around at will.
    • Limitations: Can only sense the shape of objects less than 100 lbs (44 kg), and can only affect their weight or move them for 1 hour at a time.

  • They can start to master their teleportation.
    • Limitations: They cannot teleport more than a mile between locations, and it has to be somewhere they can see or sense, or be a location they've been to before. Limited to 3 uses per Chapter.
