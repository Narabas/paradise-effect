Red Soul

Powers: Fire & Earth

Stat: Strength

Alignments: Purple & Yellow

Side Effects: Due to the temperamental and stubborn nature of Red Souls, they can be prone to emotional outbursts and extreme fits of rage, especially at higher levels. They're known to overreact and lash out in the heat of the moment.

For duration-based attacks, 5 minutes pass per post by the character(s) affected by the power. So, for instance, if Character A is using a 15 minute attack on Character B, then the effects of the attack will last until Character B has made 3 posts since being attacked by Character A's power.

Duration-based abilities that aren't attacks (ex. flying) follow these same post rules, except the 5 minutes pass per post by the user!

Level 0

(Entry Stat 0 - 1)

Powers are inward-focused.
Abilities:
  • They can heat their body to fiery temperatures at will.
    • Limitations: Cannot yet manipulate fire or earth. Can only heighten their body temperature up to 130 degrees F (54 C).

Level 1

(Entry Stat 2 - 3)

Abilities:
  • They can make sparks and small flames.
    • Limitations: They cannot make flames bigger than their paws.

  • They can manipulate fire, and make it float.
    • Limitations: They cannot manipulate more than a few pawfuls of flame at a time.

  • They can start making the ground shake, and pull it over their toes.
    • Limitations: They cannot break the ground, or mold it into any shapes. They cannot manipulate stone or metal, and cannot lift the earth into the air with their powers alone.

Level 2

(Entry Stat 4 - 5)

Abilities:
  • They can make and manipulate streams of fire.
    • Limitations: They cannot make flames bigger than they are.

  • They can create sparks by clicking their teeth together.
    • Limitations: N/A

  • They can start cracking the earth.
    • Limitations: They can only make it crack within a 5 foot (1.5 meter) radius around them.

  • They can begin to manipulate earth.
    • Limitations: They can only maneuver the earth within a 5 foot (1.5 meter) radius around them, and cannot manipulate stone or metal. They can't lift earth off the ground with powers alone, or create it from scratch, and they can only coat their claws and teeth with it.

Level 3

(Entry Stat 6 - 7)

Abilities:
  • They can make and manipulate streams of fire.
    • Limitations: They cannot make flames bigger 3x their own size.

  • They can start coating themselves in flame.
    • Limitations: They can only turn parts of themselves into flame, such as their tail or paws.

  • They can begin to generate minor earthquakes.
    • Limitations: They can only make it crack within a 10 foot (3 meter) radius around them or 25 feet (7.6 meters) straight out from them, and make gaps no bigger than 2 inches (5 cm).

  • They can manipulate earth and start to manipulate stone.
    • Limitations: They can only maneuver the earth within a 10 foot (3 meter) radius around them, and cannot manipulate metal. They can't lift earth/stone off the ground with powers alone, or create it from scratch. They can only manipulate stones and gemstones up to the size of themselves, and earth up to 3x their size. They can coat up to half of their body in earth, or just their claws and teeth in stone.

  • They can start changing earth into stones or gemstones, and vice versa, and start to make simple, rudimentary shapes with it.
    • Limitations: They can only change chunks of earth, stone, and gemstone up to half of their size or smaller.

  • They can manipulate earth and stone, and start to manipulate metal.
