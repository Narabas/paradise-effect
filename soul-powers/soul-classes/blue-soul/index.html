Blue Soul

Powers: Water & Illusions

Stat: Charisma

Alignments: Black & Green

Side Effects: Due to the capricious and cautious nature of Blue Souls, they can be prone to deep levels of mistrust and manipulation of others, especially at higher levels. They're known to change tactics on a dime, garnering them a reputation of being two-faced.

For duration-based attacks, 5 minutes pass per post by the character(s) affected by the power. So, for instance, if Character A is using a 15 minute attack on Character B, then the effects of the attack will last until Character B has made 3 posts since being attacked by Character A's power.

Duration-based abilities that aren't attacks (ex. flying) follow these same post rules, except the 5 minutes pass per post by the user!

Level 0

(Entry Stat 0 - 1)

Powers are inward-focused.
Abilities:
  • They can sense the location of flowing water.
    • Limitations: Cannot yet manipulate water or illusions. Can only sense flowing water up to 1 mile (1.6 km) away.

Level 1

(Entry Stat 2 - 3)

Abilities:
  • They can generate pools of water at their paws.
    • Limitations: The amount of water is small, no more than 2 liters.

  • They can utilize water to make bubbles or splash attacks.
    • Limitations: Their splash attacks are loose, and not firmly formed, or can cut.

  • They can begin to make small, simple illusions.
    • Limitations: These illusions last for 10 minutes max, and affect only one sense (sight, hearing, taste, touch, or smell). Their illusion must also be smaller than they are, and cannot change how they themselves look. They are limited to their own imagination and knowledge.

Level 2

(Entry Stat 4 - 5)

Abilities:
  • They can pull water from the air around them.
    • Limitations: The amount of water is around as big as they are.

  • They can utilize water to make bubbles or splash attacks.
    • Limitations: Their splash attacks are loose, and not firmly formed, or can cut. They can only be made up of as much water as they are big.

  • They can start to learn the basics of breathing underwater.
    • Limitations: They can only hold their breath for up to 15 minutes at a time.

  • They can begin to make more complicated illusions.
    • Limitations: These illusions last for 30 minutes max, and affect two senses (sight, hearing, taste, touch, or smell), though they cannot move. Their illusion can only extend in a 10 foot (3 meter) radius around themselves, and cannot change how they themselves look. They are limited to their own imagination and knowledge.

Level 3

(Entry Stat 6 - 7)

Abilities:
  • They can pull water from plants now, in addition to the air.
    • Limitations: The amount of water is akin to a 5 minute rainstorm.

  • They can now generate thick clouds around themselves, which can then be used to create a rainstorm.
    • Limitations: Can only be utilized within a 10 foot (3 meter) radius around a fixed point.

  • They can now wield their water faster, giving it a sharp edge like a blade.
    • Limitations: They can only use up to 3 "blades" at a time.

  • They can start to master breathing underwater.
    • Limitations: They can only hold their breath for up to 1 hour at a time.

  • They can begin to make more complicated illusions that can now move within their target range.
    • Limitations: These illusions last for 30 minutes max, and affect three senses (sight, hearing, taste, touch, or smell). Their illusion can only extend in a 25 foot (7.6 meter) radius around any fixed point, and cannot change how they themselves look. They are limited to their own imagination and knowledge.

Level 4

(Entry Stat 8 - 9)

Abilities:
  • They can pull water from plants and air both.
    • Limitations: The amount of water is akin to a 10 minute rainstorm.

  • They can now generate thick clouds around themselves, which can then be used to create a rainstorm. The rain can now be sharpened like shards of glass.
    • Limitations: Can only be utilized within a 15 foot (4.6 meter) radius around a fixed point.

  • They can now wield their water faster, giving it a sharp edge like a blade.
    • Limitations: They can only use up to 6 "blades" at a time.

  • They can now begin to move on top water as though it were solid ground, including that of rain.
    • Limitations: They can only walk on water for 30 minutes at a time.

  • They can start to master breathing underwater.
    • Limitations: They can only hold their breath for up to 2 hours at a time.

  • They can begin to make more complicated illusions that can now move within their target range. They are partially tangible and can be interacted with.
    • Limitations: These illusions last for 1 hour max, and affect four senses (sight, hearing, taste, touch, or smell). Their illusion can only extend in a 50 foot (15 meter) radius around any fixed point, and cannot change how they themselves look. They are limited to their own imagination and knowledge.

Level 5

(Entry Stat 10)

Abilities:
  • They can pull water from plants and air both.
    • Limitations: The amount of water is akin to a 30 minute rainstorm.

  • They can now generate thick clouds around themselves, which can then be used to create a rainstorm. The rain can be sharpened like glass.
    • Limitations: Can only be utilized within a 20 foot (6.1 meter) radius around a fixed point.

  • They can now wield their water faster, giving it a sharp edge like a blade.
    • Limitations: They can only use up to 10 "blades" at a time.

  • They can now begin to move on top water as though it were solid ground, including that of rain.
    • Limitations: They can only walk on water for 1 hour at a time.

  • They can start to master breathing underwater.
    • Limitations: They can only hold their breath for up to 3 hours at a time.

  • They can begin to make more complicated illusions that can now move within their target range. They are completely tangible and can be interacted with.
    • Limitations: These illusions last for 3 hours max, and affect all five senses (sight, hearing, taste, touch, or smell). Their illusion can only extend in a .5 mile (.8 km) radius around any fixed point, and cannot change how they themselves look. They are limited to their own imagination and knowledge.
