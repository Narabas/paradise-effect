Credits

Hey! Name's Fox or Foxi, whichever you fancy, and I go by they/them pronouns. I play Holly that Willow Branches Touch in Paradise Rising, and have been a frequent flyer in the Warrior Cats roleplay community for several, several years. The last roleplay I staffed for was created by myself, my boyfriend, Narl, and our two close friends, Okibi and Koiffee. It was a Warrior Cats roleplay we called Return to the Wild, and it was an incredibly fun, if taxing, endeavor.

When the decision was made to close it, it was mutual between the four of us. It was a lot more work than we were prepared and able to put in indefinitely. That's why, when I took some time to think of what I wanted to do after it was closed, putting together a set end campaign felt more manageable overall, and was in line with what I enjoyed most– DMing.

I was originally going to do traditional D&D, but after another Soul Class campaign came out, I reached out to Stan and asked if I'd be able to run one as well. To my surprise, she said yes!

I was ecstatic!

Despite knowing how much effort and time such a campaign would take, especially at the high number of players as I was already planning to try for, I felt confident that with multi-month interims thrown into the mix, that I would be able to handle it. Whether or not that's true remains to be seen, but I am incredibly excited to give it a try, to learn and adjust as I grow, and to attempt to create a fun campaign for everyone involved.

Please feel free to DM me with questions, concerns, or anything at all! I'm not the most chatty person you'll ever meet, but I promise I'm friendly and open to conversation, plotting and planning, and everything in between and elsewise.
