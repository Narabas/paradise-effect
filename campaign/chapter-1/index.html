Through the Looking Glass

Chapter 1




Several seconds pass with your eyes closed, and you begin to wonder when Lumi's powers will reach you. Then the sounds of strange and eerie birdsong resonates in your ears, and the scent of deep woods permeates your nose. At last you open your eyes to find--

You're nowhere you've ever been before.

Thick, gnarled trees with deadened trunks and no leaves loom over you. A chilling mist swirls around your feet, sending a ripple of unease up your spine. Remnants of old red and orange foliage crunches below you with every tentative step you take, and all around you stand all the cats that you had been with at the Hideaway-- except for Lumi. She appears soon after, drawing in quickened breaths through her nose as if she were attempting to swallow down all indications that she wanted to start panting.

"This way!" Callisto calls, and you turn. When your eyes are on him, you realize something that stands not so far behind him.

A human house, as worn and weary as the warehouse back at the Hideaway, sits in the middle of these endless woods. All around it there's a thick, concrete wall that acts as a fence, and at it's helm-- just a stone's throw from where Callisto is standing-- is a huge, iron gate. The slats seem just big enough to slip through.

Though you're not sure you want to.

"Took you long enough." A voice chimes from beyond those iron gates.

As you allow yourself to be coaxed through the gate, you half expect to see Kohaku standing there, though the voice isn't his. Lounging at the doorstep to the house is someone else that a few cats would find familiar-- Orpheus. He flicks his tail to and fro, and beside him is another cat bathed in all white fur.

"Orpheus!" Lumi snaps, though she doesn't seem angry so much as... irritated. "Where in the hell have you been?" He shakes his head, but doesn't answer, and instead rises to meet each of you now approaching the front door. The front yard is overgrown, and littered with human garbage and old cat toys. Rootsnarl's eyes are settled firmly upon the white cat, whose eyes are peculiarly split at her pupils, with one side being golden-yellow and the other being bright blue.

"Getting me, of course!" She chirps with a wave of her tail. Her gaze scours you and all of the other non-Keepers. "Oh. My. Goodness. Look at them!" The stranger seems enthusiastic. Kohaku lands somewhere above her and Orpheus, peering down with a discerning yellow gaze.

"Chasm?" Rye'wynn queries with confusion.

She answers with a nod. "Orpheus told me all about what's happening, and I'm here to help." Chasm speaks with great flourish, her whiskers quivering in delight. "It's been so long since we've seen such potential, no? After what happened last time..." Her gaze trails to Kohaku above, whose eyes narrow.

Rootsnarl pushes past the group. "That's enough. We're not here to chat." He rumbles, and Chasm laughs. "The more things change, the more they stay the same, huh, big guy?" He doesn't offer her another look, but Chasm seems hardly phased. "Yes, yes, go on and do your thing, cuties!" She adds after a moment, tail shaking in a sociable manner. "We've scouted the area and for the moment, she doesn't seem to have her eye on this place. Not sure why that is, though."

"Thank you." Lumi answers sincerely, and Rye'wynn and Callisto nod their agreement. Chasm gestures it off with a dismissive paw. "It's the least I can do!"

Rye'wynn motions towards you and every cat around you.

"The entrance is over here."

Objective: Awaken

Welcome to The Paradise Effect's very first chapter in its multi-chapter story! Here you'll find the rules, the objective, as well as all of the teams and their members listed below. Additionally, let me break down what you need to know just one more time in case anyone missed the announcements.

01

Unlike the Prologue, this Chapter does have a posting requirement! You must post at least 5 times during the course of the Chapter, or else your character will be killed and you'll be removed from the campaign. That said, there is no length requirement for posts!

If you're unable to make the 5 posts required due to life reasons, please reach out to a Staff member ASAP!

02

Every Team will have two Channels to themselves in the main server. There will be an OOC Channel for casual conversation and planning, and a Pings Channel for pinned Google Doc links and for Players to ping their team when they've posted (which may or may not include a post summary, it's completely up to you!). The only people with access to these Channels will be the team themselves, as well as the GM (Fox) and the Admins (Okibi & Narl). Make sure to heed the rules pinned in the OOC Channel!

03

Updates will come in the form of Checkpoints on each team's individual doc! If you make an action, wait until a Checkpoint has been given before posting again. Examples of actions are in the Chapter Announcement in the server. If you don't take an action, feel free to post as much as you want in between Checkpoints.

04

If your Google Doc is getting laggy after a lot of posts have been made, you can ping Fox in your team's OOC Channel to get you a new one. A new Google Doc will automatically be given for any Google Doc exceeding 50 pages.

05

Get involved and have fun! ‚ù§ As always, if you need help, please feel free to reach out to a Staff Member and we'll do our best.
